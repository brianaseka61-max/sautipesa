Privacy Policy

1. Data Collection

Sauti Pesa requires permission to read SMS notifications to provide voice confirmations for M-Pesa transactions. This data is processed locally on your device to trigger the audio alert.

2. Data Usage

Business data such as sales, inventory, and debts are stored securely. We do not sell your financial data to third parties. Your data is used solely to provide business analytics and reports within the app.

3. Permissions

The app requires Notification Access and Storage permissions to function correctly (recording sales and backing up data).

4. Security

We implement industry-standard measures to protect your information. Users are responsible for maintaining the security of their own devices.
