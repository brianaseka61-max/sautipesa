The Reality

The Hidden Leak in the Kenyan Duka

For the average Kenyan merchant, M-Pesa is both a blessing and a curse. Fake SMS fraud has become sophisticated, costing dukas thousands daily. Merchants are often too busy serving customers to verify every transaction by reading a small screen.


Furthermore, disorganized bookkeeping leads to "Invisible Profit Leaks." Forgotten credit sales (Mdeni) and unrecorded expenses mean that at the end of the month, the merchant has worked hard but has no cash to show for it.
