Who We Are

About Sauti Pesa

Sauti Pesa is a Kenyan-born FinTech solution designed to solve the everyday security challenges of the local entrepreneur. We turn your phone into a smart business partner that confirms payments by voice, so you can focus on serving customers while we watch your money.

Our Mission

To eliminate payment fraud in Kenya's informal sector and provide 1 million SMEs with simple, affordable digital accounting tools.

Our Vision

To be the leading digital voice for Kenyan commerce, turning every duka into a professional, secure financial hub.

The Problem

The Kenyan Reality

Scammers are getting smarter. Between Fake M-Pesa SMS scams and the chaos of busy shops, duka owners lose thousands daily. Checking your phone for every transaction slows you down, and manual counter-books mean you never know your actual profit.

The Value

Why Sauti Pesa?

We provide peace of mind. You no longer need to touch your phone to confirm a payment. We bring order to the chaos by digitizing your sales and debt records (Mdeni), ensuring you never lose a cent to scams or forgotten credits.
